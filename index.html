M.A.C, Minions Awareness Charity, is a charity to help out the minions. The minions deal with high-stress trying to complete the assignments given to them. It makes them all anxious and therapy will be great for them. Donate it to us to give the minions accessible high quality therapy. There is a lot of minions so we will accept all the support and donations no matter if it is only pocket change.
